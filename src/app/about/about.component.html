About ClashMultiTimer

Welcome to ClashMultiTimer, the ultimate tool designed for passionate Clash of Clans players. As a 24-year-old web developer with a keen interest in strategy games and some experience in JavaScript and open source projects, I created this platform to cater to the specific needs of CoC enthusiasts with multiple accounts.

Our goal is simple: to streamline your Clash experience. With ClashMultiTimer, you can easily organize building and lab upgrades across multiple accounts. The intuitive timers keep track of your upgrade schedules, ensuring that you never miss a beat in your quest for village supremacy.

Join us in revolutionizing the way Clash of Clans is played. With ClashMultiTimer, victory is just a few well-planned upgrades away!
